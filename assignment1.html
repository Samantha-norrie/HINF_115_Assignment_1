BMI Calculator Limitations

While BMI can be a good baseline for determining one's health, there are some limitations to the accuracy of the calculation. Individuals with a muscular build (ex. athletes) will likely receive an inflated BMI result while individuals who are older or have muscle mass may receive a deflated score (Assessing Your Weight and Health Risk, 2022).
